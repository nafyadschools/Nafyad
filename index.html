About Us

Nafyad School, a prominent private institution in Adama town, was founded in 1993 E.C. in Kebele 10 on a spacious 25,866 mÂ² compound. Initially established for Kindergarten and Grades 1-4, the school quickly gained recognition for academic excellence.

This success led to the expansion into Grades 5-8, 9-10, and eventually 11-12. With growing demand, Nafyad School opened multiple branches throughout Adama and nearby towns in Eastern Showa and West Harerghe.
