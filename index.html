About Nafyad School

Our Journey in Education Since 1993

Nafyad School is one of the most respected private educational institutions in Adama town.

Founded in 1993 E.C in Kebele 10, our flagship campus spans 25,866m¬≤ and began with a vision to provide exceptional early childhood education (KG to Grade 4).

Through our unwavering commitment to academic excellence, we expanded to offer complete primary education (Grades 5-8 in 1994 E.C), secondary education (Grades 9-10 in 1995 E.C), and preparatory education (Grades 11-12 in 1995 E.C).

Our success in Adama inspired us to establish branches across Eastern Showa and West Harerge, bringing quality education to more communities.
