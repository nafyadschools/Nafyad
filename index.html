About Us

Nafyad School, a prominent private institution in Adama town, was founded in 1993 E.C. in Kebele 10, covering a spacious area of 25,866 mÂ². Initially focused on providing quality education for Kindergarten (KG) and Grades 1-4, the school quickly gained recognition for its high commitment to academic excellence. This dedication prompted the expansion of its services, upgrading the school to include Grades 5-8, 9-10, and later 11-12.

As demand for quality education grew, Nafyad School continued to expand its reach by establishing additional branches in various kebeles of Adama Town, as well as neighboring towns in Eastern Showa and West Harerghe.

Here is a summary of the branches and their respective locations:

  • Nafyad No. 1 (KG - Grade 4): Established in 1993 E.C
  • Nafyad No. 1 (Grades 5 - 8): Established in 1994 E.C
  • Nafyad No. 1 (Grades 9 - 10): Established in 1995 E.C
  • Nafyad No. 1 (Grades 11 - 12): Established in 1995 E.C
  • Nafyad Awash Melkasa: Located in Awash Melkasa, established in 1996 E.C
  • Nafyad No. 2: Located in Kebele 04, established in 1997 E.C
  • Nafyad Bishoftu: Located in Bishoftu Town, established in 2001 E.C
  • Nafyad Gelan: Located in Gelan Town, established in 2009 E.C
  • Nafyad Olenchiti: Located in Olenchiti Town, established in 2009 E.C
  • Nafyad Chiro: Located in Chiro Town, established in 2009 E.C
